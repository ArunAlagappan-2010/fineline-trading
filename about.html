Fineline Trading is a luxury home-living and interior solutions brand that epitomises sophistication, craftsmanship and functional design. Based in Coimbatore, we curate an exclusive range of bespoke kitchens, wardrobes, living, premium furniture, bedding and luxury home décor — each crafted to merge timeless aesthetics with modern innovation. We cater to High-Net-Worth Individuals (HNWIs), affluent homeowners, luxury developers, and corporate/hospitality clients seeking world-class living environments.

The Atelier

Mastery of detail, design innovation and precision engineering. Our two-acre automated facility — nearly 60,000 sq ft — gives every project scale, consistency and uncompromising quality control.

An in-house software and design team completes full project design within 48 hours, and an entire home from design to execution in 30 days.

The Concierge

A bespoke concierge service tailored to your convenience — meet our specialised design consultants online, by phone or in person, whatever suits you best.
