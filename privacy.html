Privacy

Your privacy

This short notice explains what we collect when you use this website and how we use it. We keep it simple and we do not sell your data.

What we collect

When you submit the enquiry form we receive the name, e-mail address, phone number (if provided) and message you choose to send us. If analytics is enabled, we also collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as pages viewed and approximate region).

How we use it

We use your details solely to respond to your enquiry and to provide the products and services you ask about. We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information.

Who processes it

Form submissions are delivered to our inbox through Web3Forms, a third-party form-handling service that passes the message on to us. Any analytics data is processed by our analytics provider in aggregate form.

Retention & your choices

We keep enquiry e-mails for as long as needed to assist you and to maintain our records. You may ask us to access or delete the information you have shared at any time by writing to finelinetrading@yahoo.com.
