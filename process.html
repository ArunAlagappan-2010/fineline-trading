How we work

From your first visit to final handover, every Fineline project follows the same clear, eleven-step path — a single proven framework, adapted to your home, your space and your way of living.

Step 1

Visit Our Experience Centre

Explore a wide range of modular kitchen and wardrobe models, materials, and finishes. Get inspired by real-life setups and visualise your dream space.

Step 2

On-Site Consultation

Our design experts visit your home to assess the space, take accurate floor measurements, and understand your needs and preferences in detail.

Step 3

Material Selection

Choose from a curated collection of premium materials, finishes, colours, and hardware that best reflect your style and functionality.

Step 4

Design & Planning

Based on your inputs, we create customised 2D layouts and 3D designs. You’ll receive a complete design proposal, including layout options and finish combinations, for your review and approval.

Step 5

Quotation & Final Approval

We provide a detailed quotation along with finalised 3D designs, electrical and plumbing drawings, countertop and hardware specifications. Once approved and the payment is processed, your order is confirmed.

Step 6

Precision Manufacturing

All modular components are manufactured in our advanced facility, ensuring high-quality finishes, precision engineering, and long-lasting durability.

Step 7

Site Preparation & Delivery

Once civil work is completed as per the checklist, we deliver your finalised kitchen and wardrobe modules safely to your location.

Step 8

Professional Installation

Our trained technicians ensure a clean and accurate installation of your kitchen and wardrobes — with attention to every detail.

Step 9

Final Quality Check & Handover

We conduct a thorough quality inspection to ensure everything matches the approved design. The finished space is then handed over to you.

Step 10

Service & Usage Guidelines

We guide you on how to use and maintain your new kitchen and wardrobes. All warranty details and service support information are provided.

Step 11

Receipt & Documentation

You’ll receive all final documentation, including receipts, drawings, and warranty certificates for your records.

Design to execution in 30 days — bespoke, end to end.
